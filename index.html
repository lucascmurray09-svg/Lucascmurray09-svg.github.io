Our Mission

Community-Driven

We believe in the power of our community helping itself. Every tutor is a volunteer passionate about making a difference in their neighborhood.

100% Free

Quality education should be accessible to everyone. Dorado Tutors is completely free—no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no barriers.

Peer Learning

Students learn from peers who understand their challenges. This builds confidence, strengthens friendships, and creates a supportive learning environment.
